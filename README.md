# Sifab AS

**Supplying energy solutions that exceed expectations — your partner in specialized flow metering and more.**

Sifab AS is a specialized supplier in the Norwegian energy market, located in Bedriftsveien 24, 4313 Sandnes, Norway. We deliver flow metering solutions for custody transfer, fiscal, and process applications, along with skid fabrication, tank gauging systems, wave monitoring, and special items for offshore and onshore installations.

**Website:** [www.sifab.no](https://www.sifab.no)

## Services

- **Flow Metering** — Fiscal, custody transfer, and process metering solutions (Faure Herman turbine & ultrasonic, McCrometer V-Cone, Emerson Coriolis, Ultraflux clamp-on, orifice plates)
- **Automatic Tank Gauging** — Honeywell Enraf radar and servo tank gauges for refineries, terminals, and petrochemical facilities
- **Volume Provers & Spheres** — Small Volume Provers (SVP) with ≤0.02% uncertainty, Maloney prover spheres for offshore Norway
- **Wave Monitoring** — Radac WaveGuide 5 radar systems for real-time offshore wave data
- **Skid Fabrication & Special Items (SI)** — Norsok-compliant offshore skid manufacturing, including filtration, injection, pump, and metering skids in stainless steel (316SS, 6Mo)
- **Project Management** — Full lifecycle project management for Norsok-governed projects
- **Instrumentation & Automation** — Design, specification, and verification per Norsok I-001, I-002
- **HSE/HMS Engineering** — Hazard analysis, safety studies (HAZOP, SIL), Norsok S-001, S-002, Z-008
- **Process Engineering** — Process design, simulation, P&ID development
- **Structural Engineering** — Structural analysis and design for offshore and onshore installations

## Standards & Compliance

All work is governed by:

- **ISO 9001** — Quality management (Sifab AS is ISO 9001 certified)
- **Norsok standards** (S-001, S-002, I-001, I-002, I-104, N-001, N-004, Z-001, Z-008, Z-015, R-003, and others)
- **PED** — Pressure Equipment Directive
- **TR 2000** — Technical guidelines for pressurized systems
- **IEC 61508 / IEC 61511** — Functional safety
- **Norwegian regulations** — Petroleumstilsynet (PSA), Arbeidstilsynet, DSB, NPD/OD
