# SifabAS

**Professional Engineering Services — Norway**

SifabAS is a multidisciplinary engineering company delivering services to the Norwegian oil & gas, energy, and industrial sectors. We operate in accordance with Norsok standards and Norwegian regulatory requirements.

## Services

- **Project Management** — Full lifecycle project management for Norsok-governed projects, from feasibility through commissioning and handover.
- **Instrumentation & Automation** — Design, specification, and verification of instrument and control systems per Norsok I-001, I-002, and related standards.
- **HSE/HMS Engineering** — Hazard analysis, safety studies (HAZOP, SIL, fire & explosion), and compliance with Norsok S-001, S-002, Z-008.
- **Process Engineering** — Process design, simulation, P&ID development, and operational support per Norsok standards.
- **Structural Engineering** — Structural analysis, design, and verification for offshore and onshore installations per Norsok N-series standards.
- **Metering Engineering** — Fiscal and allocation metering system design, calibration, and uncertainty analysis per Norsok I-104 and NPD/OD regulations.
- **Customer Relations** — Dedicated follow-up ensuring deliverables meet client expectations and contractual obligations.

## Standards & Compliance

All engineering work is governed by:

- **Norsok standards** (S-001, S-002, I-001, I-002, I-104, N-001, N-004, Z-001, Z-008, Z-015, R-003, and others as applicable)
- **IEC 61508 / IEC 61511** — Functional safety
- **ISO 9001** — Quality management (SifabAS is ISO 9001 certified)
- **Norwegian regulations** — Petroleumstilsynet (PSA), Arbeidstilsynet, Direktoratet for samfunnssikkerhet og beredskap (DSB)
