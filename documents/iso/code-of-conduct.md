## Dear Employee,

This Code of Conduct is at the core of our corporate culture and defines Sifab AS's fundamental ethical values. It is built on the following commitments.

### The Way We Conduct Our Business

- We act in compliance with laws and regulations, which we treat as minimum standards, and we expect our business partners to comply with all legal requirements as well.
- We avoid situations where private interests might conflict with business interests.
- Bribery and corruption in any form are unacceptable in our business.
- We operate transparently and communicate openly with all our stakeholders.
- As a company, we are not involved in political or religious activities, nor do we make contributions to such activities.
- All year-end financial statements are audited by a reputable audit firm (Deloitte).

### We Operate With Integrity With Customers, Suppliers and Other Business Partners

- Sifab treats its business partners professionally, fairly and equally, based on objective factors and excluding personal preference or interest. We do not offer or accept personal gifts or hospitality that could create — or appear to create — improper influence.

### We Ensure a Fair Working Environment

- Every employee at Sifab is entitled to fair treatment and equal opportunity. We are committed to respecting the human rights and dignity of all employees. We do not tolerate any form of harassment or substance abuse, and we respect employees' privacy and the confidentiality of their personal data.

### We Are Committed to the Health, Safety and Security of Our People and to Protecting the Environment

- We do not make a profit at the expense of safety or security.
- Wherever we operate, we work to minimize the negative impact of our operations on the natural environment and the surrounding community.

### Closing

We can only be successful if we offer our customers the best service and are reliable partners to our suppliers. Through these clear rules of conduct, we also aim to protect you as an employee, generate value for our shareholders, and support society as a whole.

Regards,

**Tom Sverre Falch**
General Manager, Sifab AS
