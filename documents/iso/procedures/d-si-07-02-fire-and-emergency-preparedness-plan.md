## 1. Fire Instruction

- If there is a fire or possibility of a fire, call the fire department immediately: **Tel. 110**.
- Get everybody out of the building.
- Attempt to extinguish the fire using suitable equipment — a fire extinguisher is kept inside the office.
- Close doors and windows.
- Meet and inform the fire department on arrival.

## 2. Alert Plan

In case of an accident, personal injury, illness or other emergency, call AMK (Ambulance) immediately: **Tel. 113**.

- State who you are.
- State where you are.
- State what has happened.
- Hold the line until told otherwise.

## 3. Escape Plan

- Leave the building through the nearest exit.
- Everybody assembles outside, by the container at Bedriftsveien 20, for a headcount.
- Sifab AS is responsible for accounting for its own employees and sub-contractors present on site.

## 4. Responsibility

The General Manager owns this plan and is responsible for ensuring it reflects the company's current premises and that all employees and on-site sub-contractors are made aware of it. The current fire warden / HSE contact is **Sondre Falch**.
