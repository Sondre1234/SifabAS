## Purpose

This procedure ensures Sifab AS maintains an up-to-date list of approved suppliers (**D.SI.01.02 List of Suppliers**).

## Definitions

**Approved supplier** = a supplier that has been evaluated at establishment and categorized per the criteria below. Sifab AS uses four supplier categories:

- **Category A** — Suppliers of special interest / partners of Sifab AS, used regularly and approved at establishment. Includes partners where Sifab acts as representative in Norway. These suppliers shall be ISO certified.
- **Category B** — Nominated suppliers holding frame agreements with oil companies, also approved by Sifab AS. New Category B suppliers are evaluated at a regular Monday meeting and, if required, complete a supplier questionnaire before approval. Category B suppliers shall be ISO 9001 certified.
- **Category C** — Suppliers of non-critical equipment, and suppliers to the Supply House business where purchasing is controlled by customer input/requirements.
- **Category D** — Suppliers used only rarely.

## Responsibility

General Manager.

## Description

### Selection of Critical Suppliers

Critical suppliers are selected based on:

1. Ability to deliver
2. Product quality

Sifab AS's critical suppliers become close partners, working together to achieve the best possible quality and delivery time.

### Repeated Evaluation

Critical suppliers are re-evaluated once a year, during the management review, against these additional criteria:

- Deviations recorded against the supplier
- Continued ISO 9001 approval
