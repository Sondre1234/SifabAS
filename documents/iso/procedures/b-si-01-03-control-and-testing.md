## 1. Purpose

This procedure documents and demonstrates that products manufactured, fabricated or supplied by Sifab AS are controlled and tested against the specifications and requirements set out in the customer's purchase order (PO), and applicable national/international standards and regulations.

## 2. Definitions

**PO** — Purchase Order.

## 3. Scope

This procedure applies to all purchase orders where control and testing are required by standards or regulations.

## 4. Responsibility

The **Project Manager** is responsible for communicating the customer's requirements down through the project.

## 5. Description

Where the customer requires documentation regarding ordered products and services, it may be appropriate to perform control and testing at the supplier. This is then treated as Sifab's receiving control (**B.SI.01.01**).

If control and testing is not performed at the supplier, it shall be performed on receipt of the goods.

Performed control and testing shall correspond to the requirements established at contract review (**D.SI.02.02**) and shall be documented.

Where control and testing identifies defects or deficiencies, these are handled in the normal way through Sifab's deviation handling process (**D.SI.04.01**).

## 7. QA Considerations
