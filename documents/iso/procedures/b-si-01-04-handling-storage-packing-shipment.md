## 1. Purpose

This procedure ensures that handling, storage, packing, protection and delivery of Sifab AS products and goods takes place in a way that prevents damage, and that this happens in a controlled manner.

## 2. Definitions

This procedure has no definitions.

## 3. Scope

This procedure covers all handling, storage, packing, protection and delivery of products and goods within the Sifab AS system.

## 4. Responsibility

The **Project Manager**, or the individual they delegate the task to, is responsible for ensuring this procedure is followed.

## 5. Description

All material, products and equipment are handled appropriately to prevent damage.

Products for delivery are packed and protected so that they are not at risk of damage during delivery or transport. Where the customer specifies particular packaging or a dedicated packing procedure, that requirement is followed.

Transport is arranged in accordance with the purchase order.

All use of cranes, forklifts and other lifting equipment is carried out in a way that prevents damage to products or injury to personnel.

### 5.1 Delivery Documentation

A packing list always accompanies the delivery. Where the customer has its own documentation requirements, these are followed.

## 6. HSE Considerations

When moving large or heavy structures, hazards such as crush risk and the choice of lifting equipment must be considered.

## 7. QA Considerations

Avoid placing bare/shiny metal surfaces directly on the floor without protective underlay.

## 8. Environmental Considerations

None specific to this procedure.
