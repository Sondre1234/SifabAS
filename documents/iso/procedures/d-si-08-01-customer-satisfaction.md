## 1. Purpose

This procedure governs how Sifab AS measures customer satisfaction with the company's products and services.

## 2. Scope

This procedure describes how Sifab builds a picture of how customers perceive doing business with the company. It is carried out once a year, with results presented at the management review.

## 3. Responsibility

The General Manager prepares the basis for the survey. The Quality/HSEQ lead is responsible for carrying out and documenting the survey.

## 4. Description

**Step 1 — Prepare the questionnaire.** The Quality/HSEQ lead prepares the questionnaire, based on input from the General Manager. It is good practice to focus on areas where improvement opportunities may be hiding.

**Step 2 — Review responses.** The Quality/HSEQ lead reviews the responses with the General Manager. Scores above 7 are accepted without further action. Where a customer's average score is below 7, a meeting is arranged with that customer to resolve the underlying problem or misunderstanding.

**Step 3 — Report.** Based on customer responses, a concise report is prepared with recommendations for improving customer satisfaction with Sifab AS and its products and services.

**Step 4 — Management review.** The report is presented at the management review to support management's decision-making.

## 5. Form

The questionnaire used is **S.SI.01.05 Customer Survey** (English, held under S SSI - Forms).
