## Purpose

This procedure is established to support Sifab AS's purchasing objectives, both operationally and economically, and to ensure that anyone making a purchase follows a consistent, functional system.

## Definitions

**Goods** = all purchases, including services.

## Scope

This procedure covers all purchases made by Sifab AS.

## Responsibility

Anyone making a purchase on behalf of Sifab AS shall follow this procedure.

## Description

The Project Manager is responsible for all purchases by default. The exception is any purchase exceeding NOK 1,000,000, which must be raised with the General Manager.

All purchases shall be made by written order, or through the relevant ordering system carrying a serial number and job number where applicable. Every order shall state:

- Delivery date
- Delivery address
- Who placed the order
- Quantity
- Type / specification
- Special requirements, such as production specifications and/or certificates

Orders placed by telephone shall be confirmed in writing.

Approved suppliers, or suppliers nominated by the project/customer, shall be used wherever possible (ref. **S.SI.01.04 Questionnaire for Suppliers**).

The person who placed the order is responsible for ensuring the goods are delivered on time. In case of a faulty or nonconforming delivery, a deviation shall be logged in the **Deviations - NCR Tracker** so that corrective action can be taken to limit loss and delay.
