## 1. Purpose

This document defines the purpose, responsibilities, authority, qualification requirements and reporting line for each key position at Sifab AS, per ISO 9001:2015 §5.3 and referenced from **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §5.2** and **O.SI.01.01 Organization Chart**.

---

## 2. General Manager — Tom Sverre Falch

**Reports to:** Board of Directors

**Purpose:** Overall accountability for Sifab AS's operations, financial performance, and the HSEQ management system.

**Responsibilities:**
- Establish and maintain the Quality Policy and HSE Policy, and ensure the QA system is used effectively.
- Chair the annual management review and approve annual objectives and the internal audit plan.
- Approve resource allocation, investments and key supplier relationships.
- Ensure compliance with Norwegian law, NORSOK standards, PED, and requirements from PSA, Arbeidstilsynet, DSB and NPD/OD.

**Authority:** Full signing authority for the company; final approval on quotations, purchase orders, and personnel decisions.

**Qualification requirements:** Relevant technical/commercial education and demonstrated leadership experience in the energy or industrial supply sector.

---

## 3. Sales Manager — Sondre Falch

**Reports to:** General Manager

**Purpose:** Lead sales activity, customer relationships and commercial quoting for Sifab AS.

**Responsibilities:**
- Manage customer inquiries, quotations and contract review per **D.SI.02 Quotation, Contract and Order Handling**.
- Maintain and grow relationships with key accounts and channel partners (Honeywell, Faure Herman, McCrometer, Maloney, GFSA, Emerson).
- Report sales pipeline and account status at Monday meetings and the management review.
- Feed customer requirements and market intelligence into **A.SI.01.02 Context, Risks and Opportunities**.

**Authority:** Authority to issue quotations within approved pricing guidelines; escalates non-standard terms to the General Manager.

**Qualification requirements:** Commercial/technical background suited to flow metering and instrumentation sales; familiarity with the Norwegian energy market.

---

## 4. Project Manager / Engineer — Oliver Vetland

**Reports to:** General Manager

**Purpose:** Lead Sifab's project deliveries from order to completion, ensuring technical, quality and schedule requirements are met.

**Responsibilities:**
- Lead project execution, including engineering coordination, supplier follow-up and customer communication.
- Participate in sales and quotation work as needed, and prepare offers following project manager clearance.
- Gather, process and store project documentation.
- Follow up orders and deliveries from sub-suppliers per **D.SI.01 Purchasing and Supplier Approval**.
- Order goods and services within approved procedures and project budget.
- Report and follow up deviations and near-misses (RUH) in projects per **D.SI.04 Deviation Handling**.
- Participate in field work as supervisor where required.

**Authority:** Authority to place purchase orders for project-related goods and services within current procedures and approved project budget.

**Qualification requirements:** Engineering degree or equivalent experience; project management competence.

---

## 5. Service Engineer — Sayed M. Mortazavi

**Reports to:** General Manager

**Purpose:** Deliver field service, commissioning and technical support for Sifab's product range and customer installations.

**Responsibilities:**
- Perform service, commissioning, calibration and troubleshooting work at customer sites onshore (Sifab AS does not operate offshore).
- Report and follow up deviations and near-misses (RUH) encountered during service work.
- Support project delivery with technical field expertise.
- Maintain records of service work and calibration in the relevant project folder.

**Authority:** Authority to make on-site technical decisions within the scope of the service order; escalates scope changes via a VOR per **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §6.5**.

**Qualification requirements:** Relevant technical/engineering background.

---

## 6. Metering Business Consultant - UKCS — Brian Haigh

**Reports to:** General Manager

**Purpose:** Develop and follow up Sifab's UK Continental Shelf metering business, working remotely from England.

**Responsibilities:**
- Follow up Sifab's Faure Herman UK (FHNS — Faure Herman North Sea) agency business, continuing the client relationships transferred from FisCal Instrumentation.
- Follow up Sifab's Maloney agency business in the UK.
- Maintain and develop UK customer relationships, quotations and commercial follow-up in coordination with the Sales Manager.
- Feed UK market intelligence into **A.SI.01.02 Context, Risks and Opportunities**.

**Authority:** Authority to engage with UK customers and channel partners on Sifab's behalf within agreed commercial guidelines; escalates non-standard terms to the General Manager or Sales Manager.

**Qualification requirements:** Established relationships and technical/commercial background in flow metering within the UK North Sea market.

---

## 7. Common HSEQ Responsibility

All positions share the following HSEQ responsibilities, per **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §3.1**:
- Follow the QA/HSE management system in all work.
- Report deviations and near-misses (RUH) through the Deviations - NCR Tracker.
- Anyone leading or controlling the work of others is responsible for safety and environment during that work, without diminishing each individual's own safety responsibility.
