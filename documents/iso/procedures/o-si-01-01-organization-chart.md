## 1. Purpose

This document defines Sifab AS's organizational structure and reporting lines, per ISO 9001:2015 §5.3 and referenced from **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §3.1**.

## 2. Organization Structure

| Name | Title | Reports To |
|---|---|---|
| Tom Sverre Falch | General Manager | Board of Directors |
| Sondre Falch | Sales Manager | Tom Sverre Falch, General Manager |
| Oliver Vetland | Project Manager / Engineer | Tom Sverre Falch, General Manager |
| Sayed M. Mortazavi | Service Engineer | Tom Sverre Falch, General Manager |
| Brian Haigh | Metering Business Consultant - UKCS | Tom Sverre Falch, General Manager |

- The **General Manager** holds overall accountability for the company's operations, HSEQ system and quality policy, and reports to the Board of Directors.
- The **Sales Manager**, **Project Manager / Engineer**, **Service Engineer** and **Metering Business Consultant - UKCS** report directly to the General Manager and form the operational management team.
- The **Metering Business Consultant - UKCS** works remotely from England and works closely with the Sales Manager on UK Continental Shelf business (Faure Herman UK / FHNS and Maloney UK agency relationships).
- Detailed responsibility, authority and reporting relationships for each role are defined in **O.SI.02.01 Job Descriptions**.

## 3. Maintenance

This chart is reviewed at each management review (**A.SI.01.01 §7.4**) and updated whenever staffing changes occur.
