## Purpose

To ensure that all purchase orders (PO) and variation orders (VO) that Sifab AS forms with its customers and suppliers are documented and carried out per applicable rules. Quote and contract review is performed using **S.SI.01.01 Checklist for Contract Review**. A project group may additionally be formed to review specifications/requirements and advise the Project Manager / General Manager.

## Definitions

- **PO** — Purchase Order
- **VO** — Variation Order

## Scope

This procedure covers purchase orders Sifab AS holds with its customers. In principle, all orders undergo contract review to quality-assure the process and confirm Sifab is able to take on the assignment — except jobs the Project Manager judges to fall within the company's established competence and valued at or below NOK 500,000.

## Responsibility

Setting up and signing contracts with customers and suppliers is the responsibility of the General Manager / Project Manager.

- Contracts exceeding NOK 500,000: reviewed and signed by the General Manager together with the Project Manager.
- Contracts at or below NOK 500,000: reviewed and signed by the Project Manager.

## Description

When a PO/VO is received, the contents and supporting documents are checked before the order is sent to subcontractors.

The PO/VO is reviewed against **S.SI.01.01 Checklist for Contract Review**. Once reviewed and approved, an order confirmation is sent to the customer/supplier. The approved contract is then archived in the relevant project folder in Zigma360 and/or directly in Zigma360 under a unique order number.

## HSE Considerations

No special HSE considerations apply to this task.

## QA Considerations

Contract review shall be documented using the checklist wherever this procedure requires it.
