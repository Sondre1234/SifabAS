## 1. Purpose

This procedure governs final control and release of products for shipment, per NS-EN ISO 9001:2015 §8.6. It is referenced in **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §6.6** ("Release of Products and Services") and elsewhere in the technical procedures, but had no dedicated document under the pre-2026 system — this document closes that gap.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all products and packages supplied by Sifab AS — whether fabricated/assembled at Sifab's own workshop (using personnel rented from Ekke Hydraulics AS and similar partners) or sourced ready-made from manufacturers — prior to release for shipment to the customer.

## 3. Responsibility

- The **Project Manager** is responsible for scheduling final control/FAT and confirming all planned inspection and test activities are complete before release.
- **Workshop personnel** (including personnel rented from Ekke Hydraulics AS, under the Project Manager's/Service Engineer's oversight) are responsible for making the product available for final control in a completed, accessible state.

## 4. Description

### 4.1 Factory Acceptance Test (FAT)

Where required by the contract or project quality plan, a Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) is performed before shipment, in accordance with the FAT procedure/checklist agreed at contract review (**D.SI.02.02**). Customer or third-party witnessing is arranged where specified in the purchase order.

### 4.2 Verification Before Release

Before release, the Project Manager confirms:

- All control and testing per **B.SI.01.03** is complete and any nonconformities are closed per **D.SI.04.01**.
- Identification and traceability records per **B.SI.01.02** are complete for the delivered items.
- Calibration certificates for measuring equipment used during test are current and retained.
- Documentation required by the purchase order (certificates, data sheets, FAT report, calibration reports) is compiled.

### 4.3 Release Sign-Off

The Project Manager signs off release for shipment once the checks above are complete. The FAT report and/or calibration reports are stored in the project folder alongside the other delivery documentation.

### 4.4 Nonconforming Product at Final Control

If a product fails final control, it is not released; a deviation is raised per **D.SI.04.01 Handling of Nonconformities** and the product is held until the nonconformity is dispositioned.

## 5. HSE Considerations

Lifting and handling during FAT setup follows **B.SI.01.04**.
