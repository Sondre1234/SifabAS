## Purpose

To ensure the quality of quotations issued by Sifab AS.

## Definitions

None.

## Scope

This procedure covers the entire process from receiving an inquiry to issuing a completed quotation.

## Responsibility

The General Manager is responsible for establishing, implementing and ensuring this procedure is followed.

## Description

All relevant inquiries — jobs falling within Sifab's areas of operation — are registered in Zigma360. A quotation number is created and sub-folders established to retain the customer's quotation basis.

Inquiries to subcontractors are sent primarily by e-mail; offers received are stored in the corresponding project folder or directly in Zigma360, preserving the history for a possible future order. Where time does not allow gathering all prices, an experience-based estimate may be used. Prices are entered into Zigma360 to build the quotation total. For jobs that benefit from external job-tracking, additional tools may be used.

**S.SI.01.01 Checklist for Contract Review** shall always be used to confirm Sifab AS can undertake the work, except where the Project Manager judges the job falls within the company's established competence and the value does not exceed NOK 500,000.

If the quotation exceeds NOK 500,000, the checklist review shall be carried out together with the General Manager. For quotations exceeding NOK 5,000,000, the General Manager shall inform the Chairman of the Board.

After review, the quotation is sent to the customer via Zigma360, including price and delivery time, and is automatically retained under the project number in the Zigma360 workflow.

## HSE Considerations

None specific to this procedure.

## QA Considerations

None beyond the checklist requirement above.

## Environmental Considerations

Limit printed paper use by managing quotations electronically.
