## Purpose

To ensure thorough and correct order processing.

## Definitions

None.

## Scope

This procedure covers the full order process, from received order to finished product.

## Responsibility

The Project Manager is responsible for ensuring this procedure is followed. The General Manager is responsible for establishing and implementing this procedure at Sifab AS.

## Description

After receiving a purchase order, a sales order is created in Zigma360. Where a prior quotation exists, it is transferred to the sales order in Zigma360.

A contract review is carried out per **D.SI.02.02 Contract Review**, using **S.SI.01.01 Checklist for Contract Review**.

Material requirements and sub-supplies/services are established per **D.SI.01.01 Procedure for Purchasing**, and orders are placed based on the prior quotation.

An order confirmation is issued to the customer once the job is fully planned and delivery time is fixed; the order confirmation is treated as the contract between Sifab AS and the customer.

The Project Manager is responsible for execution of the job, coordinating activities, and quality-assuring processes and product — including progress tracking and documentation retention throughout, and for the final delivery, including final documentation. Deliveries are issued with a Certificate of Conformity (COC) / Declaration of Compliance (DOC).
