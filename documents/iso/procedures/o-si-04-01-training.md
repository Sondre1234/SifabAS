## 1. Purpose

This procedure describes who defines and follows up training needs at Sifab AS, per ISO 9001:2015 §7.2.

## 2. Definitions

None.

## 3. Scope

This procedure covers all training of personnel, including internal and external courses.

## 4. Responsibility

The General Manager is responsible for ensuring training is recorded in the competence overview (**A.SI.01.03 Competence Matrix**) and for training personnel in the use of Sifab's HSEQ management system.

## 5. Description

Training needs at Sifab AS are driven by:
- Market fluctuations.
- Strategic choices made by management.
- Customer requirements.
- Employees' own requests.

As a small organization that must be able to respond quickly to market changes, Sifab does not set long-term training plans. Training needs are addressed at the management review and at Monday meetings, and recorded against each employee in **A.SI.01.03 Competence Matrix**.
