## 1. Purpose

This document establishes Sifab AS's Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) Policy, referenced from **A.SI.01.01 HSEQ Handbook §3.3**. It expresses management's commitment to the health, safety and wellbeing of employees, contractors, and everyone affected by Sifab's operations.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors and activities of Sifab AS, at the company's premises (Bedriftsveien 20, 4313 Sandnes) and at customer sites onshore. Sifab AS does not operate offshore; the products Sifab supplies are subsequently installed at both offshore and onshore customer facilities, but Sifab's own personnel do not perform work at offshore installations.

## 3. HSE Policy

> **Sifab AS — HSE Policy**
>
> Sifab AS delivers flow metering, tank gauging, volume provers, wave monitoring, and Norsok-compliant skid fabrication and special items to the Norwegian energy sector. The safety of our people, our contractors, and everyone who comes into contact with our workplaces and products is our highest priority.
>
> We commit to:
>
> **Legal and Standards Compliance**
> - Comply with the Norwegian Working Environment Act (Arbeidsmiljøloven) and the Internal Control Regulation.
> - Comply with requirements from the Petroleum Safety Authority Norway (PSA), Arbeidstilsynet, DSB and NPD/OD.
> - Design and deliver equipment in accordance with the Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC, PED, ATEX and applicable NORSOK standards (S-001, S-002, Z-001, Z-008, Z-015, R-003 and others as relevant to each project).
>
> **Prevention**
> - Identify, assess and control HSE risks before work begins, using Job Safety Analysis (SJA) for non-routine or higher-risk tasks.
> - Provide a substance-free working environment (ref. **A.SI.02.04 Drugs and Alcohol Policy**).
> - Investigate incidents and near-misses to prevent recurrence, and follow up all HSE deviations through **D.SI.04 Deviation Handling**.
>
> **Competence and Involvement**
> - Ensure every employee has the training and equipment needed to work safely.
> - Involve employees in HSE planning and improvement, and expect every employee to stop work that is unsafe.
> - Hold management and supervisors accountable for the safety of the work they direct, without diminishing each individual's own safety responsibility.
>
> **Continual Improvement**
> - Set measurable HSE objectives and review them at the annual management review.
> - Report and learn from deviations and near-misses through the Deviations - NCR Tracker.
> - Review this policy at least annually to confirm it still reflects the company, its operations, and current regulatory requirements.
>
> This HSE Policy is communicated to all employees, displayed at the company's premises, and made available to interested parties on request.
>
> **Tom Sverre Falch**
> General Manager, Sifab AS

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | HSE Responsibility |
|---|---|
| General Manager | Overall accountability for the HSE Policy and system; approves annual HSE objectives |
| Project Manager | Ensures project-specific HSE requirements (customer, NORSOK, statutory) are identified and met; commissions SJA where required |
| All employees | Follow the HSE system in daily work; report deviations and near-misses; stop unsafe work |
