## 1. Purpose

This matrix records each employee's competence status and training needs, per ISO 9001:2015 §7.2, so Sifab can confirm the organization holds the competence required to deliver its products and services.

> Sifab AS does not operate offshore — all work is performed from the Sandnes office or at customer premises onshore. Offshore safety certification (GWO/BOSIET or equivalent) is therefore not tracked as a competence requirement.
>
> Sifab AS's own core staff do not personally perform welding/fabrication — that work is carried out at Sifab's workshop by personnel rented from Ekke Hydraulics AS. PED (Pressure Equipment Directive) compliance for fabricated/sold pressure equipment is tracked via supplier qualification (Ekke Hydraulics AS, D.SI.01.03) rather than as an individual competence requirement for Sifab's own staff.

Product/system training tracked historically included: FH Turbine Meters, Enraf Servo and Radar, Enraf SVP Prover, McCrometer V-Cone Meters, RADAC Wave Monitoring, and Zigma360 system training.

## 3. Maintenance

This matrix is reviewed at each management review (**A.SI.01.01 §7.4**) and updated whenever an employee joins, leaves, or completes new training. Training needs identified here feed the training plan (**O.SI.04 Training**).
