## Instructions

Send this questionnaire to new or existing suppliers/customers as part of the initial or annual evaluation under **D.SI.01.03**. Suppliers and customers of Sifab AS are expected to demonstrate standards equivalent to:

- Quality Management (ISO 9001 or equivalent)
- Occupational Health and Safety Management (ISO 45001 or equivalent)
- Environmental Management (ISO 14001 or equivalent)
- The UN Global Compact's 10 Principles for Responsible Business (unglobalcompact.org)
- Applicable sanctions regimes (UN, EU, USA, UK)
