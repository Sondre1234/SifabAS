## Numbering Scheme

Sifab AS deviation tracking uses the following numbering going forward. This register (D.SI.04-R01) is the sole system of record.

> **NCR-YYYY-NNN** — e.g. **NCR-2026-001**, **NCR-2026-002** ... resetting to 001 on 1 January each year (so the first deviation of 2027 is **NCR-2027-001**).

- The sequence starts fresh at **NCR-2026-001** — it does not continue the Zigma-era AV-count.
- The 57 historical Zigma-era entries below (AV-00001–AV-00081) remain in this register permanently under their original numbers as historical reference — they are not renumbered.
- The year-based reset makes annual deviation volume visible at a glance for the management review.
- Use the NCR Form Template (D.SI.04-F01) to raise a new deviation.
