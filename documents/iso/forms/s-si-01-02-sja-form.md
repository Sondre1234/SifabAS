## When to Use This Form

A Job Safety Analysis (SJA) shall be completed when the work meets at least one of the following criteria:

- The work is classified as hazardous and no other risk assessment has been carried out or established.
- The work is classified as hazardous and the work description deviates from a fixed procedure.
- The work is classified as hazardous and is performed rarely, or for the first time.

The SJA shall be reviewed by everyone involved before work starts.
