# Master Document List — ISO 9001 QMS, 2026 Revision

This is a continuation and consolidation of Sifab AS's existing QMS ahead of the August 2026 recertification audit — not a rewrite. Each document keeps its own revision history and continues from its last revision number (e.g. the HSEQ Handbook continues from Rev 009). Physical documents live on the shared drive under:

`HMS - KS SYSTEM SIFAB AS/ISO 9001-2015 SIFAB AS - Rev 2026/`

Status legend:
- **Done** — reissued as a controlled Word document in the new folder, revision carried forward (or issued as Rev 01 where no prior formal revision existed).
- **Done — new** — closes a genuine gap; no prior document existed, authored fresh against the ISO 9001 clause and Sifab's actual practice as described elsewhere in the QMS.
- **Done — flag** — document is filed and usable, but contains a specific open item only Tom Sverre Falch can close (a fact, an approval, a confirmation) before it's audit-ready. The flag is written directly into that document's own text/revision history, not just here.
- **Open — records** — record-keeping folder, populates progressively as records are generated; not something to "author" once.

Documents were produced 2026-07-09 through 2026-07-13. On 2026-07-13, every document was finalized for external audit review (author field changed to Sondre Falch, internal AI-drafting commentary and correction-history stripped from the documents themselves — full detail on what was flagged/corrected remains below and in each document's still-open items, just not narrated inline in the audit-facing files).
