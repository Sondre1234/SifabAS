## Policy Statement

Sifab AS is committed to maintaining a safe working environment, where the safety, health and wellbeing of its employees and everyone who comes into contact with its workplaces and property are of the highest priority.

Recognizing that drug and alcohol abuse pose a direct and significant threat to this goal, Sifab AS is committed to ensuring a substance-free working environment for all employees.

Sifab AS therefore strictly prohibits the illicit use, possession, sale, conveyance, distribution, or manufacture of illegal drugs, intoxicants, or controlled substances, in any amount or in any manner.

Sifab AS also strictly prohibits the abuse of alcohol or prescription drugs.

Any violation of this policy will result in adverse employment action, up to and including dismissal, and referral for criminal prosecution where applicable.
