# Metering Standards and Regulations — Overview

## Scope

This folder contains concise summaries of metering-specific standards and regulations relevant to Sifab AS operations in fiscal, custody transfer, allocation, and process flow metering on the Norwegian Continental Shelf and onshore installations.

## Norwegian Fiscal Metering Framework

Fiscal measurement of petroleum on the Norwegian Continental Shelf is governed by a layered regulatory and technical framework:

- **Måleforskriften (Norwegian Measurement Regulations)** — Issued by the Norwegian Petroleum Directorate (NPD / OD). The primary legal basis for all fiscal and allocation metering requirements on the NCS. Defines maximum uncertainty limits, proving requirements, approval process, and reporting to authorities.
- **NORSOK I-104** — Fiscal measurement systems for liquids. Detailed design standard referenced as a recognized norm under the Measurement Regulations (see `/standards/norsok/`).
- **NORSOK I-105** — Fiscal measurement systems for gas (see `/standards/norsok/`).
- **NPD approval** — All fiscal metering systems must be approved by the NPD before installation and first use. The operator submits a metering study; Sifab provides technical documentation to support the submission.

## Key International Standards

- **API MPMS** — Manual of Petroleum Measurement Standards. The globally accepted technical reference for measurement methods, proving procedures, volume correction calculations, and electronic metering systems. Referenced by both NORSOK I-104/I-105 and the Measurement Regulations.
- **ISO 5168 / GUM** — Uncertainty analysis framework for metering systems.
- **ISO 3171** — Automatic pipeline sampling for crude oil and liquid hydrocarbons.
- **AGA Reports** (AGA 3, 7, 9, 11) — Gas measurement standards used alongside API MPMS for gas flow metering.
