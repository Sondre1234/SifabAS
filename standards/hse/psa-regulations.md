# PSA (Petroleumstilsynet) Regulatory Framework

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Reference | Framework Regulations, Facilities Regulations, Management Regulations, Activities Regulations, Technical and Operational Regulations |
| Title | Petroleum Safety Authority Norway -- Regulations for Petroleum Activities |
| Issuing Body | Petroleumstilsynet (PSA) |
| Current Version | Consolidated editions, last major revision cycle 2023-2024 |

## Scope

The PSA regulatory framework governs health, safety, and environment for all petroleum activities on the Norwegian Continental Shelf and associated onshore facilities. The framework consists of the overarching Framework Regulations and four subordinate regulation sets. These regulations apply primarily to operators and licensees, but flow down to equipment suppliers like Sifab AS through contractual requirements and through direct obligations relating to product compliance and workplace safety.

## The Four Regulation Sets

### 1. Facilities Regulations (Innretningsforskriften)

The Facilities Regulations set technical requirements for the design and outfitting of facilities used in petroleum activities. They establish performance-based requirements and reference NORSOK standards and other recognized norms for detailed compliance.

| Section | Topic | Relevance to Sifab AS |
|---------|-------|------------------------|
| Ch. II | General requirements for facilities | Establishes that equipment shall be designed to withstand all foreseeable loads and conditions. Applies to Sifab's prover vessels and skid structures. |
| Ch. III | Structural integrity | Requirements for load-bearing structures. Relevant to skid fabrication and structural frame design. |
| Ch. IV | Piping and process equipment | Requirements for pressure-containing equipment, materials selection, and corrosion protection. Directly applicable to prover fabrication and metering skids. |
| Ch. V | Electrical installations | Requirements for electrical equipment in hazardous areas. ATEX compliance is referenced. Relevant to instrumentation on Sifab's metering systems. |
| Ch. VI | Instrumentation and automation | Requires that safety-critical instrumentation meets IEC 61508/61511. Relevant to SIL-rated instruments supplied by Sifab. |
| Ch. IX | Materials | Material selection requirements, including sour service (NACE MR0175/ISO 15156). Critical for Sifab's material procurement and fabrication. |
| References | NORSOK standards | References S-001, I-001, I-002, Z-001, M-001, M-630, M-650 as recognized norms. Sifab must comply with these when contractually required. |

### 2. Management Regulations (Styringsforskriften)

The Management Regulations establish requirements for the management systems used to ensure compliance with HSE legislation. They require a systematic approach to risk management, competence, documentation, and continuous improvement.

| Section | Topic | Relevance to Sifab AS |
|---------|-------|------------------------|
| Sec. 3-5 | Management system | Operators must have management systems; Sifab's ISO 9001 QMS satisfies the supplier-side management system expectation. |
| Sec. 6 | Management of risk | Requires systematic identification and management of risks. Sifab participates in HAZOP and risk assessments as a supplier. |
| Sec. 7 | Objectives and strategies | Requires measurable HSE objectives. Sifab's quality policy and HSE objectives align with this. |
| Sec. 9 | Acceptance criteria for major accident risk | Operators set acceptance criteria; Sifab must design equipment that enables operators to meet these criteria. |
| Sec. 11 | Competence | Requires that personnel have adequate competence. Sifab must demonstrate welder qualifications, NDE operator certifications, and engineering competence. |
| Sec. 14 | Documentation and traceability | Requires traceable documentation. Aligns with Sifab's B.SI.01.07 document handling procedure. |
| Sec. 17 | Deviation handling | Requires systematic handling of deviations, nonconformities, and corrective actions. Covered by Sifab's NCR process. |

### 3. Activities Regulations (Aktivitetsforskriften)

The Activities Regulations cover the execution of petroleum activities, including maintenance, modifications, and operational procedures. They are primarily directed at operators but affect suppliers during installation, commissioning, and hook-up phases.

| Section | Topic | Relevance to Sifab AS |
|---------|-------|------------------------|
| Sec. 22 | Handling of hazardous substances | Requirements for safe handling of chemicals and hazardous materials. Relevant during fabrication (welding gases, coating chemicals) and offshore work. |
| Sec. 24 | Working environment factors | Noise, vibration, lighting, ergonomics requirements. Applicable to Sifab's workshop operations and any offshore work. |
| Sec. 25 | Safe job analysis (SJA) | Requires risk assessment before non-routine work. Sifab personnel must participate in SJAs during offshore campaigns. |
| Sec. 33 | Lifting operations | Requirements for lifting equipment and operations (references NORSOK R-003). Relevant to skid lifting and offshore installation. |
| Sec. 46 | Maintenance | Requirements for maintenance programs. Sifab provides maintenance manuals and recommended spare parts lists with delivered equipment. |
| Sec. 48 | Work permits | Requires work permit systems for hazardous work. Sifab personnel must follow operator work permit systems when working offshore. |

### 4. Technical and Operational Regulations (Teknisk og operasjonell forskrift)

These regulations cover the technical condition and operation of facilities. They include requirements for inspection, testing, and monitoring of equipment in service.

| Section | Topic | Relevance to Sifab AS |
|---------|-------|------------------------|
| Sec. 5 | Verification | Requires independent verification of safety-critical systems. Equipment supplied by Sifab may require third-party verification (DNV, Bureau Veritas, etc.). |
| Sec. 7 | Material handling | Requirements for materials used in petroleum activities. Sifab must deliver materials with proper certificates (EN 10204 3.1/3.2). |
| Sec. 9 | Pressure testing | Requirements for pressure testing of equipment. Sifab performs hydrostatic and pneumatic testing per these requirements and ASME/EN standards. |
| Sec. 12 | Metering systems | Requirements for fiscal and allocation metering. References NPD Measurement Regulations. Critical for Sifab's metering deliveries. |
| Sec. 14 | Instrumentation and control systems | Requirements for maintenance and testing of I&C systems in service. Sifab's commissioning procedures must support these ongoing requirements. |

## Key Requirements for Equipment Suppliers

| Requirement | Description | How Sifab Complies |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Product compliance | Equipment must meet applicable directives (PED, ATEX, EMC) | CE marking, declarations of conformity, type examination certificates |
| Material traceability | Full material traceability from mill to final product | EN 10204 3.1/3.2 certificates, material tracking in QMS |
| Welding quality | Welding to approved WPS, qualified welders | WPS/WPQR per EN ISO 15614/15613, welder qualifications per EN ISO 9606 |
| NDE/NDT | Non-destructive examination per project requirements | Certified NDE operators (EN ISO 9712), procedures per NORSOK M-650 |
| Documentation | Complete documentation packages per NORSOK Z-001 | Document handling per B.SI.01.07, document register, MDR compliance |
| Testing | Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) with customer witness | FAT procedures, test protocols, punch lists |
| Preservation | Equipment preservation for transport and storage | Preservation procedures per project requirements |

## How PSA Regulations Reference NORSOK Standards

PSA regulations are performance-based, meaning they state what must be achieved but not always how. To bridge this gap, the regulations reference NORSOK standards and other "recognized norms" as acceptable means of compliance. The key linkages relevant to Sifab are:

| PSA Regulation | Referenced NORSOK | Subject |
|----------------|-------------------|---------|
| Facilities Regulations | S-001 | Technical safety |
| Facilities Regulations | I-001, I-002 | Instrumentation |
| Facilities Regulations | M-001, M-630 | Materials, material data sheets |
| Facilities Regulations | Z-001 | Documentation requirements |
| Activities Regulations | R-003 | Lifting operations |
| Management Regulations | Z-008 | Criticality analysis and maintenance |
