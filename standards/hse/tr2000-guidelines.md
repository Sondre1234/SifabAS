# TR2000 — Equinor Technical Requirements for Pressurized Systems

## Scope

TR2000 is Equinor's company-specific technical requirement document covering material selection, fabrication, welding, NDE, and testing for piping and pressure equipment on Equinor offshore and onshore installations. It supplements and tightens requirements from NORSOK, PED, and international codes. For Sifab AS, TR2000 is a binding reference on all Equinor-operated projects (e.g., Snorre A Small Volume Prover, SP-01415).

## Key Requirements for Sifab AS

- **Piping classes**: TR2000 defines alphanumeric pipe classes (e.g., A1A, D2S) specifying design pressure, design temperature, material grade, corrosion allowance, and wall thickness. Sifab must fabricate piping and skids to the pipe class stated in the contract.
- **Material quality**: Tighter chemical composition limits than standard ASTM/EN grades — e.g., CE ≤ 0.43, S ≤ 0.010%, P ≤ 0.020% for carbon steel. Duplex stainless steel requires ferrite 30–70% by image analysis, PREN ≥ 34 (22Cr) or ≥ 40 (25Cr super duplex). EN 10204 Type 3.2 certificates required for all pressure-retaining materials on Equinor projects.
- **Sour service hardness**: Maximum 22 HRC (248 HV10) for carbon steel base metal. HAZ and weld metal hardness surveys required on production welds. PWHT mandatory for carbon steel in sour service regardless of wall thickness (stricter than NACE MR0175 base requirements).
- **Sour service testing**: SSC testing per NACE TM0177 and HIC testing per NACE TM0284 may be required on parent material and weldments, in addition to NACE MR0175 / ISO 15156 baseline.
- **Welding**: WPS qualification per EN ISO 15614 with extended hardness surveys (weld metal, HAZ traverses), impact testing at low temperatures (to −46 °C for exposed Snorre A components), and macro/micro examination. Repair welding requires re-PWHT and 100% NDE of repaired area.
- **NDE extent**: 100% volumetric NDE (RT or UT) on all butt welds in sour service or high-consequence service — exceeds standard code minimums. Acceptance criteria may be stricter than base EN/ASME codes.
- **Coating**: External coating per TR2000 / NORSOK M-501; surface preparation Sa 2.5 (ISO 8501-1); DFT verification and holiday testing required. Internal coating required for specific services (seawater, chemical injection).
- **Documentation**: Full documentation package per NORSOK Z-001 and Equinor project requirements (Document Master Register, MDR). All deviations require formal deviation requests approved by Equinor.
