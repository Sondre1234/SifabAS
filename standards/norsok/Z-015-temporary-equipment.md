# NORSOK Z-015 — Temporary Equipment

## Scope
NORSOK Z-015 specifies requirements for the management, installation, and use of temporary equipment on petroleum installations. It covers temporary piping, temporary structures, temporary electrical installations, portable equipment, scaffolding, and temporary lifting arrangements. The standard addresses risk assessment, approval processes, documentation, and controlled removal of temporary equipment.
