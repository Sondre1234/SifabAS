# NORSOK M-101 — Structural Steel Fabrication

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK M-101 |
| Title | Structural Steel Fabrication |
| Edition | Ed. 5, 2011 |

## Scope

NORSOK M-101 specifies requirements for the fabrication of structural steelwork for offshore petroleum installations. It covers material handling, cutting, forming, welding, bolt assembly, dimensional tolerances, inspection, and testing. The standard applies to load-bearing structural steelwork including module frames, support structures, and secondary steel.
