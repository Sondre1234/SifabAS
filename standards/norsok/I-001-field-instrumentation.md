| Field | Value |

## Scope

NORSOK I-001 specifies requirements for selection, specification, installation, and testing of field instrumentation on offshore and onshore petroleum facilities. It covers process instruments, analytical instruments, control valves, on/off valves, and associated equipment. The standard addresses instrument selection criteria, material requirements, installation practices, and testing/commissioning.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | General requirements | Instrument material selection, tagging, and documentation — applies to all Sifab metering and instrumentation deliveries |
| 6 | Process instruments | Selection criteria for pressure, temperature, level, and flow instruments on metering skids |
| 6.6 | Flow measurement | Core clause — requirements for flow meters (turbine, ultrasonic, Coriolis, differential pressure) delivered by Sifab |
| 6.7 | Level measurement | Requirements for tank gauging instruments (Honeywell Enraf radar/servo gauges) |
| 7 | Analytical instruments | Requirements for sample systems and analysers integrated with fiscal metering |
| 8 | Control valves | Control valve requirements for metering and prover systems |
| 9 | On/off valves | Isolation and ESD valve requirements on metering skids |
| 10 | Installation | Installation requirements for instruments on Sifab-fabricated skids — mounting, tubing, wiring |
| 11 | Testing and commissioning | Factory acceptance test (FAT) and site acceptance test (SAT) requirements for instrumentation |
