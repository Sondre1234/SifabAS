## Scope

NORSOK N-001 establishes principles and requirements for the structural integrity management of load-bearing structures used in the petroleum industry. It covers design principles, material requirements, fabrication, installation, and in-service integrity management. The standard applies to fixed platforms, floating installations, subsea structures, and topside modules.
