# NORSOK S-002 — Working Environment

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK S-002 |
| Title | Working Environment |
| Edition | Ed. 5, 2018 |

## Scope

NORSOK S-002 defines requirements for the working environment on offshore and onshore petroleum facilities. It addresses noise, vibration, lighting, indoor climate, ergonomics, chemical health hazards, and physical working conditions. The standard aims to ensure that personnel can perform their tasks safely and efficiently throughout the facility lifecycle.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | Noise | Noise level requirements for metering stations and prover areas — pump noise, flow noise in pipes |
| 6 | Vibration | Vibration limits for equipment mounted on Sifab-fabricated skids (provers, flow meters) |
| 7 | Lighting | Minimum lighting levels for operator access areas on metering skids |
| 8 | Indoor climate | HVAC requirements for enclosed metering buildings or shelters |
| 9 | Chemical health hazards | Exposure limits relevant during fabrication (welding fumes, coating solvents) and operation (hydrocarbon exposure at metering points) |
| 10 | Ergonomics | Ergonomic access requirements for instrument maintenance on skids — valve handle heights, access platforms, reach distances |
| 11 | Layout and arrangement | Physical arrangement of equipment to support safe working — relevant to skid layout design |
