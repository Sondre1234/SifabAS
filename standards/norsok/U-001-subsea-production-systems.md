# NORSOK U-001 — Subsea Production Systems

## Scope
NORSOK U-001 specifies requirements for the design, fabrication, installation, testing, and operation of subsea production systems on the Norwegian Continental Shelf. It covers subsea wellheads, trees, manifolds, flowlines, control systems, intervention equipment, and associated topside interfaces. The standard addresses functional requirements, design life, material selection, pressure containment, corrosion protection, and documentation for subsea equipment.
