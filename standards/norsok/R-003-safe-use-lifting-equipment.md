# NORSOK R-003 — Safe Use of Lifting Equipment

## Scope
NORSOK R-003 defines requirements for the safe use of lifting equipment on petroleum installations, covering the full scope of lifting operations from pre-planning through execution and post-lift documentation. It addresses lift planning, risk assessment, competence requirements for personnel, equipment inspection and certification, lift categories (simple, complex, critical), and the management of crane operations, rigging, and material handling. The standard applies to all lifting operations on or in the vicinity of petroleum installations.

## Key Sections for Sifab AS
| Section | Topic | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| §5 | Lift categories | Classification of lifts as simple, complex, or critical — determines planning and approval requirements for Sifab skid installation lifts |
| §6 | Lift planning | Lift plan requirements — lift studies, weight and CoG documentation, rigging arrangements for Sifab-fabricated equipment |
| §7 | Risk assessment | Risk assessment for lifting operations — mandatory for complex and critical lifts of metering skids, provers, and large SI items |
| §8 | Personnel competence | Competence requirements for crane operators, riggers, and lift supervisors involved in Sifab equipment installation |
| §9 | Equipment inspection | Pre-use inspection and periodic inspection of lifting equipment — cranes, slings, shackles, spreader bars, lifting attachments |
| §10 | Rigging and slinging | Requirements for rigging configurations, sling angles, and load distribution — relevant to Sifab-supplied lifting lugs and spreader frames |
| §11 | Crane operations | Crane operation requirements, communication, exclusion zones — governs offshore installation of Sifab-supplied skids |
| §12 | Documentation | Lifting operation documentation — lift plans, inspection records, permits to work for all Sifab installation lifts |

- **Secondary**: Structural Agent — design of lifting lugs, spreader beams, and pad eyes on Sifab skids and SI items in compliance with R-003 and R-002; HSE Agent — risk assessment for lifting operations; review of client lift plans for Sifab equipment installation
