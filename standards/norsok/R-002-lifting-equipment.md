# NORSOK R-002 — Lifting Equipment

## Scope
NORSOK R-002 specifies requirements for the design, manufacture, documentation, testing, marking, and certification of lifting equipment used on petroleum installations. It covers cranes, hoists, davits, monorails, lifting accessories (slings, shackles, hooks, eye bolts), and integrated lifting attachments permanently attached to structures and equipment. The standard defines minimum design factors, material requirements, weld inspection, proof load testing, and traceability requirements for all lifting equipment.

## Key Sections for Sifab AS
| Section | Topic | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| §5 | Design requirements | Design factor of safety and load case requirements — applicable to Sifab-designed lifting lugs, pad eyes, and trunnions on skids and SI items |
| §6 | Material requirements | Material selection, toughness, and traceability for lifting components — aligns with Norsok M-001 material selection for offshore environments |
| §7 | Welding | Welding requirements for lifting attachments — weld procedure qualification, NDT acceptance criteria for lifting lug welds |
| §8 | Testing and proof loading | Proof load test requirements and documentation — Sifab must proof test and certify lifting lugs on skids before dispatch |
| §9 | Inspection and NDT | Non-destructive testing requirements for lifting equipment components and welds |
| §10 | Marking and identification | Marking requirements for safe working load (SWL), identification, and certification number on all lifting attachments |
| §11 | Documentation and certification | Certification documentation requirements — test certificates, material traceability, and conformity declarations for lifting equipment |
| §12 | Lifting accessories | Requirements for slings, shackles, and hooks supplied or used by Sifab during FAT or included with deliverables |
