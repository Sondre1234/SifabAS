| Field | Value |

## Scope

NORSOK L-001 specifies requirements for piping and valve design, material selection, fabrication, and testing for petroleum installations. It covers piping classes, material specifications, wall thickness calculations, valve selection, flange ratings, and testing requirements. The standard applies to all metallic process, utility, and fire water piping systems.
