# NORSOK E-001 — Electrical Installations

## Scope
NORSOK E-001 specifies requirements for the design, installation, testing, and documentation of electrical systems on petroleum installations. It covers power generation and distribution, motor control, lighting, heat tracing, earthing and bonding, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), area classification interfaces, and cable systems. The standard aligns with IEC standards and Norwegian regulations while addressing the specific demands of offshore and onshore petroleum facilities.

## Key Sections for Sifab AS
| Section | Topic | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| §5 | Design basis | Electrical design basis requirements — voltage levels, frequency, earthing philosophy; defines interface conditions for Sifab electrical hook-up |
| §6 | Area classification | Hazardous area classification (Zone 0, 1, 2) — directly affects selection of all electrical equipment on Sifab metering skids and ATG systems |
| §7 | Power distribution | Power supply and distribution requirements — defines how Sifab-supplied instruments and control systems are powered from facility distribution boards |
| §8 | Motor control | Motor starter and drive requirements — relevant for pump-based provers and any motor-driven equipment on Sifab skids |
| §9 | Earthing and bonding | Earthing and bonding requirements for skids, instruments, and Ex equipment — Sifab must provide earthing points and bonding connections |
| §10 | Heat tracing | Electrical heat tracing requirements for process piping and instruments — relevant for metering skids in cold environments or cold-start applications |
| §11 | Lighting | Lighting requirements for equipment rooms and outdoor installations — relevant for walk-on metering skids and shelter designs |
| §12 | Cable systems | Cable selection, routing, and installation requirements — cable schedules and cable support design for Sifab instrument and power cabling |
| §13 | UPS systems | Uninterruptible power supply requirements for critical instrumentation — relevant for custody transfer metering systems and SAS interfaces |
| §14 | Testing and commissioning | Electrical testing requirements — insulation resistance, continuity, functional tests for Sifab FAT and SAT |
| §15 | Documentation | Electrical documentation requirements — single line diagrams, cable schedules, area classification drawings for Sifab deliverables |

- **Primary**: Instrumentation Agent — primary user; applies E-001 for electrical system design and area classification interface on metering skids and ATG systems; ensures all instrument and control system electrical design meets Ex requirements for the classified area
- **Secondary**: Project Manager — electrical documentation scope in MDR; Structural Agent — cable tray routing, conduit supports, and earthing lug provisions on Sifab-fabricated skid structures; HSE Agent — area classification review and verification that Sifab equipment is suitable for the classified zone
