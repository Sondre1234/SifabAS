| Field | Value |

## Scope

NORSOK I-105 specifies requirements for fiscal (custody transfer) measurement systems for hydrocarbon liquids including crude oil, condensate, and NGL. It covers metering station design, flow meter selection, proving systems, sampling, flow computers, and uncertainty analysis. This is a core standard for Sifab AS — directly applicable to their fiscal liquid metering, prover, and tank gauging deliveries.
