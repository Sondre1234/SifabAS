# NORSOK M-630 — Material Data Sheets for Structural Steel

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK M-630 |
| Title | Material Data Sheets for Structural Steel |
| Edition | Ed. 7, 2020 |

## Scope

NORSOK M-630 provides standardized material data sheets (MDS) for structural steel products used in offshore petroleum installations. Each MDS specifies the material grade, mechanical properties, chemical composition limits, testing requirements, and documentation requirements for a specific product form (plate, section, hollow section, pipe, forging, casting).

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | General requirements | Overall requirements for material procurement — inspection document types (EN 10204 3.1/3.2), marking, traceability |
| MDS Y04 | Plates, S355 | Standard structural steel plates for skid frames — most commonly used grade in Sifab fabrication |
| MDS Y05 | Plates, S420 | Higher-strength structural steel plates for heavily loaded skid frames |
| MDS Y06 | Plates, S460 | High-strength plates for weight-critical offshore modules |
| MDS Y10 | Sections, S355 | Structural sections (I-beams, channels, angles) for skid frames |
| MDS Y20 | Hollow sections | Structural hollow sections (SHS, RHS, CHS) used in skid bracing and supports |
| MDS Y30 | Seamless pipes | Structural pipe for tubular members |
| MDS Y40 | Forgings | Forged components for critical structural connections |
