# NORSOK M-001 — Materials Selection

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK M-001 |
| Title | Materials Selection |
| Edition | Ed. 6, 2014 |

## Scope

NORSOK M-001 defines requirements for the selection of materials for process components, piping, and structural elements in petroleum production facilities. It covers material selection based on corrosion resistance, mechanical properties, temperature requirements, and fabricability. The standard provides material selection tables for different service conditions.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |

| 5 | General requirements | Material selection philosophy — corrosion allowance, design life, material documentation requirements |

| 9 | Nickel alloys | Inconel and other nickel alloys for high-temperature or highly corrosive metering applications |

| 12 | Welding consumables | Matching welding consumable requirements for selected base materials |
| 14 | Corrosion-resistant alloys (CRA) | CRA selection for sour service, CO2 service — relevant to fiscal metering in corrosive well fluids |
