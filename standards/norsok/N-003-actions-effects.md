# NORSOK N-003 — Actions and Action Effects

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK N-003 |
| Title | Actions and Action Effects |
| Edition | Ed. 3, 2017 |

## Scope

NORSOK N-003 defines actions (loads) and action effects (responses) to be used in the structural design of offshore petroleum installations. It covers permanent actions, variable actions, environmental actions (wind, wave, current, ice), accidental actions (fire, explosion, dropped objects, ship collision), and load combinations for different limit states.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | Permanent actions | Dead weight of equipment, piping, and structural steel on Sifab skids — weight reports and COG calculations |
| 6 | Variable actions | Live loads for maintenance access, laydown areas on skid platforms, operational loads |
| 7 | Environmental actions | Wind, wave spray, and temperature loads on topside-mounted metering skids |
| 8 | Accidental actions | Dropped object loads, fire loads, and explosion overpressure on skid structures |
| 9 | Deformation actions | Thermal expansion effects on piping and structural connections — relevant to prover pipe design |
| 10 | Load combinations | ULS, SLS, ALS, and FLS load combinations for structural design of skids |
| 11 | Transportation loads | Transport accelerations for skids during marine transport and lifting — critical for modular delivery |
