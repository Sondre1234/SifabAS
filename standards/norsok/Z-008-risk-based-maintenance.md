# NORSOK Z-008 — Risk Based Maintenance and Consequence Classification

## Scope
NORSOK Z-008 establishes a methodology for risk-based maintenance planning and consequence classification of equipment on petroleum installations. It covers equipment criticality assessment, maintenance strategy selection (preventive, condition-based, corrective), spare part classification, performance standards, and maintenance performance monitoring. The standard enables operators to optimize maintenance effort based on the consequence of equipment failure.

| §8 | Spare parts | Spare part classification and stocking recommendations — critical and insurance spares list for metering systems |
