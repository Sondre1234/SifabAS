| Field | Value |

## Scope

NORSOK S-001 establishes requirements and guidelines for technical safety on offshore and onshore petroleum installations on the Norwegian Continental Shelf. It covers fire and explosion protection, escape and evacuation, active and passive fire protection systems, and area classification. The standard applies to all phases from design through decommissioning.
