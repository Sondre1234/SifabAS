## Scope

NORSOK N-006 provides requirements and guidance for the assessment of structural integrity of existing offshore load-bearing structures. It covers reassessment triggers, assessment methods, acceptance criteria, and mitigation measures. The standard addresses situations where structures are subjected to changed conditions such as increased loads, extended service life, or identified damage.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
