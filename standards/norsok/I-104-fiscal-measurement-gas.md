# NORSOK I-104 — Fiscal Measurement Systems for Hydrocarbon Gas

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

| Title | Fiscal Measurement Systems for Hydrocarbon Gas |
| Edition | Ed. 4, 2015 |

## Scope

NORSOK I-104 specifies requirements for the design, fabrication, installation, commissioning, operation, and maintenance of fiscal (custody transfer) measurement systems for hydrocarbon gas. It covers metering station design, flow meter selection, flow computer requirements, gas chromatograph systems, proving/calibration, and uncertainty analysis. This is a core standard for Sifab AS fiscal gas metering deliveries.
