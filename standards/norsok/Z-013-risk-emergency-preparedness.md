# NORSOK Z-013 — Risk and Emergency Preparedness Assessment

## Scope
NORSOK Z-013 defines requirements for risk assessment and emergency preparedness analysis for petroleum activities onshore and offshore. It covers quantitative risk analysis (QRA), qualitative risk assessment methods, emergency preparedness analysis (EPA), design accidental loads, risk acceptance criteria, and ongoing risk management across the facility lifecycle. The standard integrates hazard identification, frequency analysis, consequence modelling, and emergency response planning.

## Key Sections for Sifab AS
| Section | Topic | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| §5 | Risk assessment methodology | Overall risk assessment framework — Sifab equipment must be designed to support facility QRA requirements |
| §6 | Hazard identification (HAZID) | HAZID methodology for metering systems — hydrocarbon leak scenarios, overpressure, ignition sources |
| §7 | Frequency analysis | Leak frequency data for flanges, valves, instruments — relevant to metering skid pipe class and leak detection design |
| §8 | Consequence analysis | Consequence modelling for metering area — fire, explosion, and toxic gas dispersion from high-pressure hydrocarbon leaks |
| §9 | Design accidental loads | Fire and explosion loads and dropped object loads affecting Sifab skid structural and equipment design |
| §10 | HAZOP | HAZOP study requirements for metering, prover, and ATG systems — process safety review methodology |
| §11 | SIL assessment | Safety integrity level determination for safety functions — ESD valves, high-pressure trips, on Sifab-supplied systems |
| §13 | Emergency preparedness | Emergency response requirements affecting metering skid layout — escape routes, TR, access for emergency response |
