| Field | Value |

## Scope

NORSOK P-001 establishes requirements for the process design of offshore and onshore petroleum production facilities. It covers process design basis, pressure-temperature ratings, process simulations, safety and relief systems, and process documentation. The standard applies to separation, processing, metering, export, and utility systems.
