# NORSOK M-601 — Welding and Inspection of Piping

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Standard | NORSOK M-601 |
| Title | Welding and Inspection of Piping |
| Edition | Ed. 6, 2016 |

## Scope

NORSOK M-601 specifies requirements for welding and inspection of piping systems for offshore and onshore petroleum installations. It covers welding procedure specifications (WPS), welder qualifications, production welding, NDT, and acceptance criteria. The standard applies to process piping, utility piping, and piping components including metering spools, prover assemblies, and pressure-containing equipment.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | Welding procedure specification | WPS requirements for metering spools, prover pipe, and piping on Sifab skids — qualification testing (WPQR) |
| 6 | Welder qualification | Welder and welding operator qualification requirements — Sifab must maintain qualified welders for all material combinations |
| 7 | Production welding | Requirements during production — preheat, interpass temperature, heat input control, post-weld heat treatment (PWHT) |
| 8 | Carbon steel welding | Specific requirements for carbon steel piping welds — typical for prover pipes and non-corrosive service metering spools |
| 9 | CRA and clad welding | Requirements for duplex, super duplex, and clad material welding — critical for corrosive service metering systems |
| 10 | NDT requirements | Non-destructive testing extent and methods (VT, PT, MT, UT, RT) for piping welds — varies by piping class and service |
| 11 | Acceptance criteria | Weld acceptance criteria — imperfection limits for different quality levels |
| 12 | Repair welding | Repair procedures for defective welds — repair limitations and re-inspection requirements |
| 13 | Documentation | Welding records — WPS, WPQR, welder certificates, weld logs, NDT reports, heat treatment records |
