| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

| Title | Environmental Care |

## Scope

NORSOK S-003 establishes requirements for environmental risk management and pollution prevention on Norwegian petroleum installations. It covers discharge to sea, emissions to air, waste management, and environmental monitoring. The standard applies to design, construction, operation, and decommissioning phases.

## Key Clauses for Sifab AS

| Clause | Topic | Relevance to Sifab |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 5 | Environmental risk management | Risk assessment requirements for metering systems handling hydrocarbons — leak prevention and containment |
| 6 | Discharges to sea | Containment and drainage design for metering skids — drip trays, drain systems for provers |
| 7 | Emissions to air | Fugitive emission considerations for metering system flanges, valves, and connections |
| 8 | Waste management | Waste handling during skid fabrication (steel scrap, coating waste, welding consumables) |
| 9 | Chemical use and discharge | Chemical handling at metering and prover operations (calibration fluids, cleaning agents) |
| 10 | Environmental monitoring | Monitoring interfaces that Sifab instrumentation systems may need to support |
