# NORSOK M-501 — Surface Preparation and Protective Coating

| Field | Value |

| Edition | Ed. 7, 2022 |

## Scope

NORSOK M-501 defines requirements for surface preparation and protective coating of offshore and onshore petroleum installations. It covers coating system selection, surface preparation grades, application methods, inspection, and repair. The standard specifies coating systems for different exposure categories (atmospheric, splash zone, submerged, insulated surfaces).
